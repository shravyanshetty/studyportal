About Study Portal

Study Portal is a dedicated platform designed to help students access academic resources in one place. Instead of searching through scattered messages or WhatsApp groups, students can now easily find and download:

  • Previous year question papers
  • CIE question papers

My goal is to save students' time and reduce stress during exam preparation by providing a well-organized, branch-wise and year-wise collection of resources. Whether you're revising at midnight or just getting started, Study Portal is always here to support your academic journey- anytime,anywhere.
